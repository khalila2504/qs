ARCTIC MONKEYS

Arctic Monkeys are an English rock band formed in Sheffield in 2002.
The group consists of Alex Turner (lead vocals, guitar),
Jamie Cook (guitar, keyboards), Nick O'Malley (bass guitar, backing vocals),
and Matt Helders (drums, backing vocals).

THE 1975

The 1975 are an English pop rock band formed in 2002 in Wilmslow, Cheshire.
Now based in Manchester, the band consists of lead vocalist and rhythm guitarist
Matthew "Matty" Healy, lead guitarist Adam Hann, bassist Ross MacDonald,
and drummer George Daniel.
